The Web Home of Dennis M. Sponer

Dennis is an entrepreneur based in Las Vegas, Nevada. He is an attorney licensed to practice in Nevada and California. Dennis obtained his Juris Doctorate from Brigham Young University and his Masters in Tax Law (L.L.M.) from the University of San Diego School of Law. Dennis also has his Masters of Business Administration (M.B.A.) through TRIUM, a program jointly administered by the London School of Economics, NYU's Stern School of Business and HEC Paris.

SRX Advisors

After seventeen years of amazing growth and success, Dennis sold ScripNet to HealthCare Solutions and founded SRX Advisors. SRX Advisors provides consultancy services in the areas of prescription medication management, insurance coverage, workers' compensation, payment and reimbursement processing and entrepreneurship. Dennis' 20+ years of business experience as well as his legal training has helped some of the largest companies in the nation optimize their health care spending.

ScripNet, Inc.

Dennis co-founded ScripNet as a workers' compensation focused pharmacy benefit management company in 1997. ScripNet served as the payment and reimbursement intermediary between insurance payors and pharmacies. It's network of over 60,000 pharmacies provided prescription medications to ScripNet's numerous clients which included some of the largest employers and carriers in the nation. Clients included the States of Texas and Nevada, the Cities of Philadelphia and Atlanta, Starbucks, Lockheed Martin and Federal Express, among others.
